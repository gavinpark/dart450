2000

Pussy Palace

On September 15th, 2000 male police officers barged into Club Toronto, a bathhouse hosting an all-women event dubbed Pussy Palace by the organizers. Many of the attending women were naked or semi-naked and were shocked by the police officer’s surprising and unwarranted raid. The officers searched rooms, women’s personal effects and took names and addresses.

Pussy Palace

This sparked outrage and within weeks of the raid, community members gathered and protested outside of the 52 Division police station, this demonstration came to be known as the ‘Panty Picket’.
